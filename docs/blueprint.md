# **App Name**: Anubhuti

## Core Features:

- Multi-Modal Emotional Analysis: Utilize sentiment analysis (via tool use), facial expression recognition, and voice tone detection at the home page to offer users an analysis of their present mental state. Provides a baseline.
- Student Mental Health Survey: Conduct mental health survey (PHQ-9, GAD-7, GHQ) with graphical level analysis of anxiety and depression based on user survey.
- Risk Management and Suicide Prevention: Trigger mental health helpline and coping strategies if the app detects a mental health crisis.
- AI-Guided First-Aid Support: Interactive chatbox that offers coping strategies (via tool use) and refers students to professionals when needed.
- Psychoeducational Resource Hub: Offer videos, relaxation audio, mental wellness guides in regional languages, curated based on survey results.
- Mental Health Session Scheduling: Student mental health reviews are collected and saved, in order to provide progress tracking.
- Confidential Counselor Booking: Students may book appointments with on-campus counselor or mental health helpline, with complete privacy.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm saffron (#FF9933), evokes optimism and energy.
- Background color: Light cream (#FAF9F6), creates a calming atmosphere.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#468F8F) offers a modern touch.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a balance of warmth and clarity.
- Simple, culturally resonant icons representing emotional states.
- Clean, card-based layout with clear visual hierarchy for easy navigation.
- Subtle transitions and micro-interactions to gamify user experience.
